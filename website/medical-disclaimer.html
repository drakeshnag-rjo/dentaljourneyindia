Not Medical Advice

The content on this website, including treatment descriptions, pricing information, and health-related content, is provided for informational purposes only. It is not intended to be a substitute for professional dental or medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always seek the advice of a qualified dentist or healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding a dental or medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you have read on this website.

Treatment Information

Treatment descriptions, success rates, and recovery timelines mentioned on this website are general guidelines and may vary significantly based on individual patient factors including overall health, oral health condition, bone density, age, and other medical considerations.

The treating dentist at the partner clinic will provide you with specific information about your treatment plan, risks, alternatives, and expected outcomes based on your individual clinical assessment.

Pricing Estimates

All prices displayed on this website are estimates only. Actual treatment costs may differ based on clinical assessment, the complexity of your case, additional procedures needed (such as bone grafting or extractions), the specific implant brand or materials chosen, and the treating clinic's current pricing.

Final pricing will be confirmed by the treating clinic after evaluation. We recommend obtaining a detailed, written treatment plan with itemized costs before committing to any procedure.

AI Communications

Our Telegram bot and website chat widget use artificial intelligence to answer questions and provide information. These AI systems are designed to be helpful and informative, but they are not a substitute for professional dental consultation. AI responses may contain inaccuracies, and treatment-related decisions should always be made in consultation with a qualified dental professional.

No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Using this website or communicating with our AI chatbot does not create a doctor-patient or dentist-patient relationship. A clinical relationship is established only when you engage directly with a treating dentist at a partner clinic.

Risks of Dental Treatment

All dental procedures carry inherent risks, including but not limited to infection, bleeding, nerve damage, implant failure, allergic reactions to materials, and complications from anesthesia. These risks exist regardless of where treatment is performed.

Dental treatment abroad carries additional considerations including limited ability for in-person follow-up with the treating dentist, differences in regulatory frameworks between countries, and the need to coordinate post-treatment care with a local dentist in your home country.

Travel and Insurance

We strongly recommend that you purchase comprehensive travel insurance that specifically covers medical and dental treatment abroad, medical evacuation, and trip cancellation or interruption. Review your insurance policy carefully to understand what is and is not covered.

Your Responsibility

By using our services, you acknowledge that the decision to undergo dental treatment abroad is your own, that you will perform your own due diligence on any clinic before committing to treatment, that you will obtain a detailed treatment plan and provide informed consent before any procedure, and that you will disclose your complete medical history to the treating dentist.
